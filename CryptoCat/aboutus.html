What is cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital form of money based on cryptography. It utilizes blockchain technology to ensure security, transparency, and decentralization. Cryptocurrency has no physical representation and exists only in digital form. It provides the opportunity for fast and secure financial transactions, as well as opens new avenues for investments and innovations.
